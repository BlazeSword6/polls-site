Step 1: Configure

Upload your engagement information by filling in the required fields: Country, Audit title, and Audit objectives. Once completed, click the Submit button to proceed.

Step 2: Generate Controls

After submitting your engagement information, double-click on the key business risk item that appears to automatically generate the risk control matrix below.

Step 3: Customize Analysis

Use the various toolkit options to customize your analysis. You can upload supporting documents, select multiple criteria, set date ranges, and configure analysis parameters. Once you've made your selections, click Apply Settings to update your risk control matrix.
